Earning Points

Earn points by selling eligible American Standard, DXV and GROHE products, and entering sales during the collection period. Go to www.MyEnjoyRewards.com and follow the links and instructions to either register or enter your unique User Id and Password (if you already have registered). After registering and logging in, click on the "My Sales" button and follow the instructions. Members will earn points for each applicable sale during the collection period.

LWTA reserves the right to change the points awarded for purchases at any time during the Program. At various times during the Program, LWTA will declare "promotional" periods.  Members who sell products and enter claims during the Promotional period will earn additional points set forth on the Enjoy (more) Rewards Program website. The website will indicate whether there is an active promotional period in effect.

Sales can only be entered 1 time.

Members may not combine or transfer points with other members' accounts. Members may not combine points obtained by others for a deposit into a single member's account, nor transfer, sell, or otherwise dispose of points in any manner in violation or attempted subversion of these Terms and Conditions.  Any attempt to combine or transfer points will result in disqualification from the Program and forfeiture of all points in any member's account. LWTA reserves the right to change, add or remove the methods by which members can earn and collect points.

Once a member files a claim, the validation of the claim and potential points earned will be subject to verification by a team of auditors whose decisions on the outcome are in its sole discretion and are final and binding. Points will not be awarded until after the verification process is complete. Claims are void if (a) not obtained in accordance with these Terms and Conditions and through legitimate channels or (b) any part deemed to be found is counterfeit, altered, defective, tampered with or irregular in anyway. The corresponding points will appear on the member's Enjoy (more) Rewards Reloadable MasterCard within 10-15 business days after completion of the validation process. However, LWTA reserves the right in its sole discretion to award points later than 15 business days if additional time is needed for any reason.
